W05 Project: Website Planning Document

Site Name: Kindred Critters - This site represents my 11 year old daughter's pet sitting business in Tremonton, Utah.

Site Purpose: For my daughter's pet sitting business - This site provides all the information needed for pet sitting care, including services offered, contact information, and background information for the owner.

Scenarios: What kind of pet sitting do you offer? What is the contact information to schedule pet sitting? Who is the person who would be taking care of my pet?

Color Schema: #3d5a80 blue east bay (RGB: 61, 90, 128), #e0fbfc baby blue (RGB: 224, 251, 252), #ee6c4d burnt sienna (RGB: 238, 108, 77)

Fonts: Headings & subheading - Oswald Paragraph & content - Roboto
